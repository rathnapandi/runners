• FitTogether Help
  • Welcome to FiTogether app. You will be able to link your Strava activities to be automatically counted towards the Axway's Health & Wellness Week leaderboard.
  • You are here. You are logged in, and you have used your Axway ID. Now lets select the event and register
  • Click on the Events button on the navigation bar and select the 'Health and Wellness Week' event for participating in Axway's event for 2022
  • Now, you are allowed to create a registration and link your Strava account
  • During the entire event allow Strava to be connected to the app to count your activities towards the leaderboard
  • Select your department you represent within Axway for the stripes and the timing of the event is already preselected for you
  • Once you are done with the registration, and connecting your strava account - you are READY to GO for the Health & Wellness Week.
  • Sit back & Relax! Use the FiTogether teams channel and jive posts to participate in the event and interact with your fellow axwegians
  • Stay tuned for more updates on the leaderboard and have fun competing with your colleagues
